Hunted House

description:

In a small town, deep within their local forest, was a popular and broken house. Many rumors came from the house, with many unspeakable thinks taking place to those who decide to explore. Only fate will know what will happen to the victims of the house.

Production:

This was the first time I made a small comedy skit in Premire Pro. In Grade 9, me, Marcus and JJ made small horror skits and learned how to use filters. This project showed me how to make an improvised script and learn the basics of editing videos
