"Who's the Class Clown Now?"

"Who's the Class Clown Now?", or Class Clown for short, is a small comedic interview podcast series about the host (me) interviewing other students around the school! As of now there are two podcasts, one with MK about transfering to another school, and with Marcus about Boys Club.

This podcast has showed me how to clean audio and create scripts for interviews. As well as building my confidence to talk to more people and get use to my voice.

Episode 1

This episode is about transferring schools with MK Tran, a prior transfer student who transferred to Sutherland a year ago. This interview will show how changing schools is stressful and scary but can be fun and easy when you get used to new schools.

Episode 2

This episode is about Boys Club in Sutherland with Marcus Groot, a member of Boys Club, who rejoined this year in Grade 11. This interview shows how clubs can change and variety from member to member, as well as influencing others to be in a club.
